PEOPLE NOT NUMBERS

Our website is a work in process :)

Art by Carolina Echeverria

People not Numbers is a Concordia student-lead project that aims to introduce a new approach to the academic community and Montreal. Our project is comprised of a panel that discusses immigration and an interactive gallery about immigration. We believe in the importance of rekindling human connection and changing popular narratives in regards to this 'issue'.

Join us in invisioning the word integration and empowerment contexualized!
